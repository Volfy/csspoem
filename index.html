Our story begins on earth, many futures away.
All civilizations are now dust.
Nonetheless, humanity has persisted.
Let's focus on one small group of adventurers: Their time is spent scavenging the ruins of old cities, hacking together a town for themselves, passing time playing salvaged computer games, riding dune-buggies, and speculating endlessly about the meaning of extant diaries whose authors are now long dead.
To survive in the harsh environment that greets them is not simple. Still, to this group of scavvies, for them, the end never feels nearby.
Llevashirym, a city outside their general reach, had been heralded as one of the most prosperous and creative societies. The depths of information referred to by authors that'd lived nearby, was sure to be useful - and more importantly, quench their endless curiosities about the world before them.
Before they'd even be able to visit, they'd have much to prepare. The resources necessary for a journey like this, would require many days of work.
However, the scavvies do not give up, and they find hope in all endeavours. The rough directions and vague descriptions could only help so far, their eyes open to anything that could be.
