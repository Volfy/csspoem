Our story begins on the Earth, many futures away. Mass desertification has turned all but a few civilizations to dust, but humanity has persisted nonetheless. A group of adventurers - they call themselves scavvies - spend their free time hiking old cities, scavenging for parts to hack together all kinds of things. Their finds help build up shelter and is essential to their survival in this new world, but they are not desperate.
